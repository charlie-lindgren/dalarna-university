# AB1014

[Kursplan på du.se →](https://www.du.se/sv/utbildning/kurser/kursplan/?code=AB1014)

- **Poäng:** 10 högskolepoäng
- **Nivå:** Grundnivå 1
- **Ämnestillhörighet:** Arbetsvetenskap (ABA)

## Lärandemål

På en övergripande nivå är kursens mål att studenten efter avklarad kurs ska:   
- visa förmåga att relatera relevant forskning och teorier inom ledarskap till egna erfarenheter   
- kunna reflektera över ett ledarskap för ökat resultat i företaget   
- ha utvecklat en personlig utvecklingsplan för sitt ledarskap   
  
Detta innebär att studenten efter genomgången kurs ska kunna:   
- leda ett samtal för konfliktlösning   
- kommunicera så att konflikter undviks   
- coacha, ge konstruktiv feedback och positiv förstärkning   
- leda beslutsmöten, informationsmöten och kreativa möten på ett effektivt sätt   
  
Studenten ska även kunna diskutera, analysera och reflektera över hur:   
- relationen mellan medarbetare och chef påverkar ledarskapet   
- trygghet och förtroende skapar förutsättningar för bra dialog på arbetsplatsen   
- intressemotsättningar kan omvandlas till samarbete och skapa långvariga relationer   
- attraktivt arbete och medarbetarskap påverkas av och påverkar ledarskapet   
- motivation och engagemang skapas hos medarbetarna

## Innehåll

Kursen består av sex avsnitt.
 

I avsnitt 1 "Kursupptakt", presenteras kursens filosofi, innehåll och upplägg. Deltagarna delger sina erfarenheter och förväntningar på kursen och presenteras genom kommunikationsövningar. Teorier presenteras och reflektioner görs om ledarskap för ökat resultat.

## Examinationsformer

Examination sker i form av aktivt deltagande vid obligatoriska övningar, uppgifter och gemensamma kurstillfällen, samt individuella skriftliga reflektionsprotokoll. Muntlig examination kan förekomma i särskilda fall efter beslut av examinator.

## Arbetsformer

Praktiska övningar, hemuppgifter och föreläsningar. Övningsuppgifter på arbetsplatser genomförs löpande under kursen.
 

Kursens pedagogik bygger på deltagarnas aktiva medverkan i övningar på kursdagar och på egna arbetsplatser, utbyte av erfarenheter och reflektioner. Bearbetning och redovisning av lärandet sker genom att deltagarna löpande skriver reflektionsprotokoll. I dessa ska behandlas litteratur, föreläsningar, övningar, utbytet mellan deltagarna, övningsuppgifter på arbetsplatser, samt egen arbetslivserfarenhet.
 

Reflektionsprotokollen ska, för den studerande, fungera som grund för ett under kursen successivt utvecklat aktivt agerande (verbaliserat eller konkret).

## English Version

### Learning Outcomes

The overall objective is that the student on completion of this course should:   
- demonstrate an ability to relate relevant research and theories in leadership to his/her own experiences   
- be able to reflect on leadership for increased company results   
- have developed a personal development plan for his/her leadership   
  
This means that the student shall be able to   
- lead talks to solve conflicts   
- communicate so conflicts are avoided   
- coach, give constructive and positive feed-back   
- lead decision, information and creative meetings in an efficient way   
  
The student shall also be able to discuss, analyze and reflect over how   
- the relation between co-workers and the boss influences leadership   
- trust and confidence creates the conditions for a good dialog in the workplace   
- conflicts of interest can be transformed to cooperation and create long-term relationships  
- workplace morale and co-working are affected by and themselves affect leadership  
- motivation and devotion are instilled in co-workers

### Course Content

The course consists of 6 parts.   
  
In part 1 “Course start “, the course philosophy, content and schedule are presented. The participants talk about their experiences and expectations and present themselves through communication exercises.   
Theories are presented and reflections made on leadership for increased company results.   
  
In part 2 “Developing leadership“ is carried out according to a model developed by Swedish National Defence College. A theoretical model for developing leadership is presented. Each participant makes personal feedback in the form of a 360 -degree inventory and makes a personal development plan for their own leadership.  
  

In part 3 “Communication with and within groups“ theories are presented and reflections made on communication that creates trust. The participants train with practical tools how trust and confidence for dialog is created; conflicts of interest are transformed into cooperation and create long-term relationships; leading talks can solve conflicts; different meetings can be lead efficiently. 

In part 4 “Co-workers and motivation“ theories are presented and reflections made on Attractive Work and co-working as a base for internal motivation and engagement. The participants train with practical tools how motivation and devotion are instilled; coaching, constructive and positive feed-back can be given; groups are developed; leadership can be carried out in the process of change.   
  
In part 5 “Personal development plan“ a follow-up is made of Developing Leadership from part 2. Work is done with the personal development plan. Theories are presented and reflections made based on the personal development plans.   
  
Part 6 “The world around“ deals with leadership from perspectives from outside, close to and inside the company. Theories are presented and reflections made on how co-workers can be energised for their work; new forms for transferring competence and experiences and development of a customer oriented organization that understands creative processes.

### Assessment

The assessment for this course consists of active participation during compulsory exercises, assignments and group activities, as well as individual written reflections. Oral examinations can occur in special cases - to be decided by the examiner

### Forms of Study

Forms of study consist mainly of practical exercises, assignments and lectures. Training assignments in the workplace are carried out throughout the course.  
The pedagogical method is based on the student’s active participation in exercises and at the workplace, to promote the exchange of experiences between the students in addition to written reflections. The analysis and presentation of individual learning is achieved by regularly writing individual “reflection protocols.” These will include discussions of literature, lectures, exercises, the exchange of experiences, training assignments at the workplace and individual work-life experiences.  
  
The reflection protocols will work as a base for student development throughout the course (verbalized and concrete).
